# Monetization

**Decision: Open-source**

No billing, no feature gating, no usage metering.

**Why:** The goal is a developer tool with good DX and extensibility, not a revenue product. Open-source removes billing infrastructure from scope entirely.

**Impact on scope:**
- No Stripe, no usage tracking, no tier enforcement
- Focus goes to developer experience and extensibility
